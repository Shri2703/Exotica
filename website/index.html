About Us

About the College

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution, the main objective of the KGiSL Institute of Technology is to provide industry-embedded education and to mold the students for leadership in industry, government, and educational institutions; to advance the knowledge base of the engineering professions; and to influence the future directions of engineering education and practice.

Department of IT

The B.Tech Information Technology Department focuses on equipping students with essential skills for the dynamic tech landscape. Our curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications across various fields, including software development, networking, and cybersecurity. Experienced faculty foster critical thinking and innovation through hands-on projects and internships. We promote research collaboration and provide industry connections to enhance career opportunities. Our goal is to nurture both technical proficiency and essential soft skills, preparing students to become leaders in the technology sector.
